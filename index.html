Privacy Policy

Bhopal Bar Association

Effective Date: 31-01-2025

Bhopal Bar Association respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information of advocates and users of our mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information.

Data Collection and Use

Our app provides a public directory of advocates in Bhopal. We do not collect, store, or share any personal user data. The advocate profiles displayed in the app are sourced from publicly available data. No sensitive legal information, user accounts, or financial transactions are processed within the app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not collect any personal information from users of the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Maintain and manage advocate records.
  • Provide access to legal resources and networking opportunities.
  • Improve app functionality and user experience.
  • Send important updates, notifications, or legal announcements.
  • Ensure compliance with regulatory and legal obligations.
  • Enhance security and prevent fraudulent activities.
  • Personalize user experience and provide tailored content.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • With Consent: When you provide explicit permission.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, court order, or government regulations.
  • Service Providers: To trusted third parties assisting in app maintenance, hosting, or security.
  • Business Transfers: In case of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and regular audits.

5. User Rights

  • Access and update your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Restrict or object to certain processing activities.
  • Request data portability, where applicable.

To exercise these rights, contact us at connectshikshatech@gmail.com.

The app may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

8. Children's Privacy
